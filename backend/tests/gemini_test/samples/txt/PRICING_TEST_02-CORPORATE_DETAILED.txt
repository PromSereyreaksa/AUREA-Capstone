   - Print-ready PDF with bleed

2. Brochure Series (4 types)
   - Tri-fold format
   - Product catalog, student banking, SME services, premium wealth management
   - 6 pages each (front + back of 3 panels)
   
3. Counter Display Materials (3 sets)
   - Table tents for promotional offers
   - Wobbler designs
   - Shelf talkers

CATEGORY B: Digital Marketing Assets
1. Email Newsletter Templates (2 templates)
   - HTML format, mobile responsive
   - One for promotions, one for educational content
   
2. Social Media Templates (15 templates)
   - Facebook: 5 post templates
   - Instagram: 5 feed + 5 story templates
   - LinkedIn: company update format
   - Dimensions per platform specifications
   
3. Website Banner Set (8 banners)
   - Hero banners for main pages
   - Promotional banner variations
   - Responsive sizes: desktop, tablet, mobile

CATEGORY C: Corporate Stationery Updates
1. Business Card Redesign
   - Standard and executive variants
   - Front and back design
   - For approval by board before printing

2. Letterhead Template
   - MS Word template format
   - Official correspondence use

3. PowerPoint Master Template
   - 15 slide layouts minimum
   - Corporate presentation standard

CATEGORY D: Promotional Items Design
1. Merchandise Graphics (printing outsourced separately)
   - Branded notebook cover design
   - Tote bag graphic
   - Calendar design (12 months + cover)
   
2. Event Materials
   - Booth backdrop design (3m x 2.5m)
   - Roll-up banner (85cm x 200cm)
   - Certificate template for financial literacy workshops
